01. The Sounds

They don't bark. They wheek. It is an ear-piercing scream reserved for the sound of a refrigerator opening.

(Click this card to hear it!)

02. The Popcorn

When joy overtakes their bodies, they jump uncontrollably. This is known as "popcorning." It looks like a tiny, happy seizure.

03. The Socialite

Guinea pigs are social animals. Keeping a solo pig is actually illegal in Switzerland. They need a gossip partner.

04. The Sleepers

Guinea pigs sleep with their eyes open. Nightmare fuel, but normal. They close their eyes when sleeping only in a safe environment.

05. The Teeth Situation

Their teeth never stop growing. Ever. This is why they chew constantly—hay, wood, hopes, dreams. Without gnawing, their teeth would overgrow and turn them into tiny medieval weapons.

06. The Clean Freaks

Guinea pigs groom themselves constantly and even clean each other. They look polite, but it’s less “spa day” and more “if I don’t lick you right now, society collapses.”
